Pie

Description

A pie is a baked dish which is usually made of a pastry dough casing that contains a filling of various sweet or savoury ingredients.

Sweet pies may be filled with fruit (as in an apple pie), nuts (pecan pie), brown sugar (sugar pie), sweetened vegetables (rhubarb pie), or with thicker fillings based on eggs and dairy (as in custard pie and cream pie).

Savoury pies may be filled with meat (as in a steak pie or a Jamaican patty), eggs and cheese (quiche) or a mixture of meat and vegetables (pot pie).

Ingrediants

    2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

    4 teaspoons sugar

    1/4 teaspoon fine salt

    14 tablespoons cold butter, diced

    1 large egg, lightly beaten with 2 tablespoons cold water

Instructions

    Make the dough by hand. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.

    Make the dough in a food processor. With the machine fitted with the metal blade, pulse the flour, sugar, and salt until combined

    Form the dough into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate until thoroughly chilled, at least 1 hour.

    Enjoy =D
